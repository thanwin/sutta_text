3. Sattivaggo
1. Sattisuttaṃ
21. Sāvatthinidānaṃ . 
Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi –
‘‘Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho, ḍayhamānova matthake;
Kāmarāgappahānāya, sato bhikkhu paribbaje’’ti.
‘‘Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho, ḍayhamānova matthake;
Sakkāyadiṭṭhippahānāya, sato bhikkhu paribbaje’’ti.
