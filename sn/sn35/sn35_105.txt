2. Upādāyasuttaṃ
105. ‘‘Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati kiṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkha’’nti?
‘‘Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe….
‘‘Cakkhusmiṃ kho, bhikkhave, sati cakkhuṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhaṃ …pe… 
manasmiṃ sati manaṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhaṃ. 
Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, cakkhu niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā’’ti?
‘‘Aniccaṃ, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā’’ti?
‘‘Dukkhaṃ, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkha’’nti?
‘‘No hetaṃ bhante’’ …pe….
‘‘Jivhā niccā vā aniccā vā’’ti?
‘‘Aniccā, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā’’ti?
‘‘Dukkhaṃ, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkha’’nti?
‘‘No hetaṃ, bhante’’ …pe….
‘‘Mano nicco vā anicco vā’’ti?
‘‘Anicco, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā’’ti?
‘‘Dukkhaṃ, bhante’’.
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkha’’nti?
‘‘No hetaṃ, bhante’’.
‘‘Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati …pe… 
manasmimpi nibbindati. 
Nibbindaṃ virajjati; 
virāgā vimuccati; 
vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. 
‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī’’ti. 
Dutiyaṃ.
