3. Sabbavaggo
1. Sabbasuttaṃ
23. Sāvatthinidānaṃ . 
‘‘Sabbaṃ vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi. 
Taṃ suṇātha. 
Kiñca, bhikkhave, sabbaṃ? Cakkhuñceva rūpā ca, sotañca saddā ca, ghānañca gandhā ca, jivhā ca rasā ca, kāyo ca phoṭṭhabbā ca, mano ca dhammā ca – 
idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sabbaṃ. 
Yo, bhikkhave, evaṃ vadeyya – 
‘ahametaṃ sabbaṃ paccakkhāya aññaṃ sabbaṃ paññāpessāmī’ti, 
tassa vācāvatthukamevassa ; 
puṭṭho ca na sampāyeyya, uttariñca vighātaṃ āpajjeyya. 
Taṃ kissa hetu? 
Yathā taṃ, bhikkhave, avisayasmi’’nti. 
Paṭhamaṃ.
