8. Chavisuttaṃ
177. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati …pe… 
‘‘dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasiloko , bhikkhave, chaviṃ chindati, chaviṃ chetvā cammaṃ chindati, cammaṃ chetvā maṃsaṃ chindati, maṃsaṃ chetvā nhāruṃ chindati, nhāruṃ chetvā aṭṭhiṃ chindati, aṭṭhiṃ chetvā aṭṭhimiñjaṃ āhacca tiṭṭhati. 
Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko …pe… 
evañhi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabba’’nti. 
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
