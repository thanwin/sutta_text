15. Diṭṭhivaggo
1. Ajjhattasuttaṃ
150. Sāvatthinidānaṃ . 
‘‘Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhadukkha’’nti? 
Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe… 
‘‘rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati rūpaṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhadukkhaṃ. 
Vedanāya sati …pe… 
saññāya sati … 
saṅkhāresu sati … 
viññāṇe sati viññāṇaṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhadukkhaṃ. 
Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā’’ti? 
‘‘Aniccaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā’’ti? 
‘‘Dukkhaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhadukkha’’nti? 
‘‘No hetaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Vedanā …pe… 
saññā … 
saṅkhārā … 
viññāṇaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā’’ti? 
‘‘Aniccaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ, dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā’’ti? 
‘‘Dukkhaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhadukkha’’nti? 
‘‘No hetaṃ, bhante’’. 
‘‘Evaṃ passaṃ …pe… 
nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti. 
Paṭhamaṃ.
