9. Upādāniyasuttaṃ
121. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. 
‘‘Upādāniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi upādānañca . 
Taṃ suṇātha. 
Katame ca, bhikkhave, upādāniyā dhammā, katamaṃ upādānaṃ? 
Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, upādāniyo dhammo, yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha upādānaṃ.
