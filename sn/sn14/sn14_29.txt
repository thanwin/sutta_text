Sattamaṃ.
Sattannaṃ suttantānaṃ uddānaṃ –
Asamāhitaṃ dussīlaṃ, pañca sikkhāpadāni ca;
Satta kammapathā vuttā, dasakammapathena ca;
Chaṭṭhaṃ aṭṭhaṅgiko vutto, dasaṅgena ca sattamaṃ.
Kammapathavaggo tatiyo.
