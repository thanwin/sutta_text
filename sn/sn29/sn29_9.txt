9. Tatiyasutasuttaṃ
350. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. 
Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca – 
‘‘ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā saṃsedajānaṃ nāgānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjatī’’ti? …Pe…
ayaṃ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṃ paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā saṃsedajānaṃ nāgānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjatīti.
Navamaṃ.
