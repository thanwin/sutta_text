8. Dutiyarukkhasuttaṃ
538. ‘‘Seyyathāpi , bhikkhave, ye keci devānaṃ tāvatiṃsānaṃ rukkhā, pārichattako tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; 
evameva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ – 
bodhāya. 
Katame ca, bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyā dhammā? 
Saddhindriyaṃ , bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. 
Vīriyindriyaṃ …pe… 
satindriyaṃ …pe… 
samādhindriyaṃ …pe… 
paññindriyaṃ bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. 
Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave , ye keci devānaṃ tāvatiṃsānaṃ rukkhā, pārichattako tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; 
evameva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ – 
bodhāyā’’ti. 
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
