3. Micchattavaggo
1. Micchattasuttaṃ
21. Sāvatthinidānaṃ . 
‘‘Micchattañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammattañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha. 
Katamañca , bhikkhave, micchattaṃ? 
Seyyathidaṃ – 
micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāsamādhi . 
Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchattaṃ. 
Katamañca, bhikkhave, sammattaṃ? 
Seyyathidaṃ – 
sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāsamādhi. 
Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammatta’’nti. 
Paṭhamaṃ.
