3. Anissukīsuttaṃ
296. ‘‘Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjati. 
Katamehi pañcahi? 
Saddho ca hoti, hirimā ca hoti, ottappī ca hoti, anissukī ca hoti, paññavā ca hoti – 
imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjatī’’ti. 
Tatiyaṃ.
