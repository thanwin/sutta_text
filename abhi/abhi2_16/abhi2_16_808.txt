9. Navakaniddeso
808. Tattha katamā navasu anupubbavihārasamāpattīsu paññā? 
Paṭhamajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, dutiyajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, tatiyajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, catutthajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, ākāsānañcāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, viññāṇañcāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, ākiñcaññāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, saññāvedayitanirodhasamāpattiyā vuṭṭhitassa paccavekkhaṇāñāṇaṃ – 
imā navasu anupubbavihārasamāpattīsu paññā. 
Evaṃ navavidhena ñāṇavatthu.
Navakaṃ.
