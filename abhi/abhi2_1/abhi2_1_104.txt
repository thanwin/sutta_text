104. Ekavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
cittasampayutto.
Duvidhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
atthi hetu ceva hetusampayutto ca, atthi hetusampayutto ceva na ca hetu.
Tividhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
atthi saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko, atthi asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko, atthi asaṃkiliṭṭhaasaṃkilesiko …pe…. 
Evaṃ dasavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho.
