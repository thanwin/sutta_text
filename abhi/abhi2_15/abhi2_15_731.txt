731. Catasso paṭisambhidā – 
atthapaṭisambhidā, dhammapaṭisambhidā, niruttipaṭisambhidā, paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā.
Katame dhammā akusalā?

somanassasahagataṃ diṭṭhigatavippayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena …pe… 
upekkhāsahagataṃ diṭṭhigatasampayuttaṃ …pe… 
upekkhāsahagataṃ diṭṭhigatasampayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena …pe… 
upekkhāsahagataṃ diṭṭhigatavippayuttaṃ …pe… 
upekkhāsahagataṃ diṭṭhigatavippayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena …pe…

domanassasahagataṃ paṭighasampayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena …pe…

upekkhāsahagataṃ uddhaccasampayuttaṃ rūpārammaṇaṃ vā …pe… 
dhammārammaṇaṃ vā yaṃ yaṃ vā panārabbha, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti. 
Ime dhammā akusalā. 
Imesu dhammesu ñāṇaṃ dhammapaṭisambhidā. 
Tesaṃ vipāke ñāṇaṃ atthapaṭisambhidā. 
Yāya niruttiyā tesaṃ dhammānaṃ paññatti hoti tatra dhammaniruttābhilāpe ñāṇaṃ niruttipaṭisambhidā. 
Yena ñāṇena tāni ñāṇāni jānāti – 
‘‘imāni ñāṇāni idamatthajotakānī’’ti, ñāṇesu ñāṇaṃ paṭibhānapaṭisambhidā.
