713. Katame dhammā sikkhā? 
Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti …pe… 
arūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti …pe… 
lokuttaraṃ jhānaṃ bhāveti niyyānikaṃ apacayagāmiṃ diṭṭhigatānaṃ pahānāya paṭhamāya bhūmiyā pattiyā vivicceva kāmehi …pe…
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṃ dandhābhiññaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti. 
Ime dhammā sikkhā.
Abhidhammabhājanīyaṃ.
