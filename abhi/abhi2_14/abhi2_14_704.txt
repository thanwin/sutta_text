704. Tattha katamaṃ pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ? 
Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti somanassasahagataṃ ñāṇasampayuttaṃ pāṇātipātā viramantassa, yā tasmiṃ samaye pāṇātipātā ārati virati paṭivirati veramaṇī akiriyā akaraṇaṃ anajjhāpatti velāanatikkamo setughāto – 
idaṃ vuccati ‘‘pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ’’. 
Avasesā dhammā veramaṇiyā sampayuttā.
Tattha katamaṃ pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ? 
Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti somanassasahagataṃ ñāṇasampayuttaṃ pāṇātipātā viramantassa, yā tasmiṃ samaye cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ – 
idaṃ vuccati ‘‘pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ’’. 
Avasesā dhammā cetanāya sampayuttā.

idaṃ vuccati ‘‘pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ’’ .
