1013. Kāmadhātuyā upapattikkhaṇe kassa tayo hetū pātubhavanti? 
Kāmāvacarānaṃ devānaṃ, paṭhamakappikānaṃ manussānaṃ, gabbhaseyyakānaṃ sattānaṃ sahetukānaṃ ñāṇasampayuttānaṃ upapattikkhaṇe tayo hetū pātubhavanti – 
alobho vipākahetu, adoso vipākahetu, amoho vipākahetu. 
Kāmadhātuyā upapattikkhaṇe etesaṃ ime tayo hetū pātubhavanti.
Kāmadhātuyā upapattikkhaṇe kassa dve hetū pātubhavanti? 
Kāmāvacarānaṃ devānaṃ, paṭhamakappikānaṃ manussānaṃ, gabbhaseyyakānaṃ sattānaṃ sahetukānaṃ ñāṇavippayuttānaṃ upapattikkhaṇe dve hetū pātubhavanti – 
alobho vipākahetu, adoso vipākahetu. 
Kāmadhātuyā upapattikkhaṇe etesaṃ ime dve hetū pātubhavanti. 
Avasesānaṃ sattānaṃ ahetukā pātubhavanti.
