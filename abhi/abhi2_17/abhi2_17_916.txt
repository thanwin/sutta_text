(8) Tisso taṇhā
916. Tattha katamā tisso taṇhā? 
Kāmataṇhā, bhavataṇhā, vibhavataṇhā.
Tattha katamā bhavataṇhā? 
Bhavadiṭṭhisahagato rāgo sārāgo cittassa sārāgo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘bhavataṇhā’’.
Tattha katamā vibhavataṇhā? 
Ucchedadiṭṭhisahagato rāgo sārāgo cittassa sārāgo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘vibhavataṇhā’’. 
Avasesā taṇhā kāmataṇhā.
Tattha katamā kāmataṇhā? 
Kāmadhātupaṭisaṃyutto rāgo sārāgo cittassa sārāgo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘kāmataṇhā’’.
Rūpadhātuarūpadhātupaṭisaṃyutto rāgo sārāgo cittassa sārāgo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘bhavataṇhā’’.
Ucchedadiṭṭhisahagato rāgo sārāgo cittassa sārāgo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘vibhavataṇhā’’. 
Imā tisso taṇhā.
