(8) Antavādiṭṭhi ca anantavādiṭṭhi ca
898. (Ka) tattha katamā antavādiṭṭhi? 
‘‘Antavā attā ca loko cā’’ti – 
yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ …pe… 
vipariyāsaggāho – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘antavādiṭṭhi’’.
(Kha) tattha katamā anantavādiṭṭhi? 
‘‘Anantavā attā ca loko cā’’ti – 
yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ …pe… 
vipariyāsaggāho – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘anantavādiṭṭhi’’.
