(6) Bhavadiṭṭhi ca vibhavadiṭṭhi ca
896. (Ka) tattha katamā bhavadiṭṭhi? 
‘‘Bhavissati attā ca loko cā’’ti – 
yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ …pe… 
vipariyāsaggāho – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘bhavadiṭṭhi’’.
(Kha) tattha katamā vibhavadiṭṭhi? 
‘‘Na bhavissati attā ca loko cā’’ti – 
yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ …pe… 
vipariyāsaggāho – 
ayaṃ vuccati vibhavadiṭṭhi.
