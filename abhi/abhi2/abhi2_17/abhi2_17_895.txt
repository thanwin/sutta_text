(5) Avijjā ca bhavataṇhā ca
895. (Ka) tattha katamā avijjā? 
Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ …pe… 
avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘avijjā’’.
(Kha) tattha katamā bhavataṇhā? 
Yo bhavesu bhavacchando bhavarāgo bhavanandī bhavataṇhā bhavasineho bhavapariḷāho bhavamucchā bhavajjhosānaṃ – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘bhavataṇhā’’.
