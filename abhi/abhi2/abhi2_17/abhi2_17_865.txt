(48) Lābhena lābhaṃ nijigīsanatā
865. Tattha katamā lābhena lābhaṃ nijigīsanatā? 
Lābhasakkārasilokasannissito pāpiccho icchāpakato ito laddhaṃ āmisaṃ amutra harati amutra vā laddhaṃ āmisaṃ idha āharati, yā evarūpā āmisassa eṭṭhi gaveṭṭhi pariyeṭṭhi esanā gavesanā pariyesanā – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘lābhena lābhaṃ nijigīsanatā’’.
