(17) Sīlavipatti ca diṭṭhivipatti ca
907. (Ka) tattha katamā sīlavipatti? 
Yo kāyiko vītikkamo vācasiko vītikkamo kāyikavācasiko vītikkamo – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘sīlavipatti’’. 
Sabbampi dussīlyaṃ sīlavipatti.
(Kha) tattha katamā diṭṭhivipatti? 
‘‘Natthi dinnaṃ, natthi yiṭṭhaṃ …pe… 
ye imañca lokaṃ parañca lokaṃ sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā pavedentī’’ti – 
yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ …pe… 
vipariyāsaggāho – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘diṭṭhivipatti’’. 
Sabbāpi micchādiṭṭhi diṭṭhivipatti.
