707. Tattha katamaṃ surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ? 
Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti upekkhāsahagataṃ ñāṇavippayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā viramantassa, yā tasmiṃ samaye cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ – 
idaṃ vuccati ‘‘surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ’’.
