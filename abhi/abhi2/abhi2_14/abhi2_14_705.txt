705. Tattha katamaṃ pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ? 
Yasmiṃ samaye kāmāvacaraṃ kusalaṃ cittaṃ uppannaṃ hoti upekkhāsahagataṃ ñāṇavippayuttaṃ sasaṅkhārena pāṇātipātā viramantassa, yā tasmiṃ samaye cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṃ – 
idaṃ vuccati ‘‘pāṇātipātā veramaṇī sikkhāpadaṃ’’. 
Avasesā dhammā cetanāya sampayuttā.
