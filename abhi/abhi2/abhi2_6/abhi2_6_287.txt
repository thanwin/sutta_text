287. Tattha katamā avijjā? 
Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ adassanaṃ …pe… 
avijjālaṅgī moho akusalamūlaṃ – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘avijjā’’ …pe… 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso’’.
Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā? 
Yaṃ cetasikaṃ asātaṃ cetasikaṃ dukkhaṃ cetosamphassajaṃ asātaṃ dukkhaṃ vedayitaṃ cetosamphassajā asātā dukkhā vedanā –
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘phassapaccayā vedanā’’.
Tattha katamaṃ vedanāpaccayā paṭighaṃ? 
Yo cittassa āghāto …pe… 
caṇḍikkaṃ asuropo anattamanatā cittassa – 
idaṃ vuccati ‘‘vedanāpaccayā paṭighaṃ’’ .
Tattha katamo paṭighapaccayā adhimokkho? 
Yo cittassa adhimokkho adhimuccanā tadadhimuttatā – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘paṭighapaccayā adhimokkho’’.
Tattha katamo adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo? 
Ṭhapetvā adhimokkhaṃ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo’’ …pe… 
tena vuccati ‘‘evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī’’ti.
