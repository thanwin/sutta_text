692. Tattha katamā mettā? 
Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi …pe… 
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti. 
Ime dhammā kusalā. 
Tasseva rūpāvacarassa kusalassa kammassa katattā upacitattā vipākaṃ vivicceva kāmehi …pe… 
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye metti mettāyanā mettāyitattaṃ mettācetovimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘mettā’’. 
Avasesā dhammā mettāya sampayuttā.
Tattha katamā mettā? 
Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā …pe… 
dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ , tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti. 
Ime dhammā kusalā. 
Tasseva rūpāvacarassa kusalassa kammassa katattā upacitattā vipākaṃ vitakkavicārānaṃ vūpasamā …pe… 
dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ …pe… 
tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ …pe… 
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ …pe… 
dutiyaṃ jhānaṃ …pe… 
tatiyaṃ jhānaṃ …pe… 
catutthaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye metti mettāyanā mettāyitattaṃ mettācetovimutti –
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘mettā’’. 
Avasesā dhammā mettāya sampayuttā.
