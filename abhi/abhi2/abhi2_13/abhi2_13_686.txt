686. Tattha katamā karuṇā? 
Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi …pe… 
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati karuṇāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye karuṇā karuṇāyanā karuṇāyitattaṃ karuṇācetovimutti –
ayaṃ vuccati ‘‘karuṇā’’. 
Avasesā dhammā karuṇāya sampayuttā.
