118. Ekavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
cittasampayutto.
Duvidhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
atthi saṃyojanañceva saṃyojanasampayutto ca, atthi saṃyojanasampayutto ceva no ca saṃyojanaṃ.
Tividhena saṅkhārakkhandho – 
atthi atītārammaṇo, atthi anāgatārammaṇo, atthi paccuppannārammaṇo …pe…. 
Evaṃ dasavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho.
