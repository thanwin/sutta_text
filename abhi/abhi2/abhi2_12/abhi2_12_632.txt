5. Arūpāvacaravipākā
632. Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye arūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti sabbaso ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ samatikkamma nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasaññāsahagataṃ sukhassa ca pahānā …pe… 
catutthaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti. 
Ime dhammā kusalā. 
Tasseva arūpāvacarassa kusalassa kammassa katattā upacitattā vipākaṃ sabbaso ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ samatikkamma nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasaññāsahagataṃ sukhassa ca pahānā …pe… 
catutthaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye duvaṅgikaṃ jhānaṃ hoti – 
upekkhā, cittassekaggatā. 
Idaṃ vuccati ‘‘catutthaṃ jhānaṃ’’. 
Avasesā dhammā jhānasampayuttā.
