2. Arūpāvacarakusalaṃ
626. Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye arūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti sabbaso ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ samatikkamma nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasaññāsahagataṃ sukhassa ca pahānā …pe… 
catutthaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye duvaṅgikaṃ jhānaṃ hoti – 
upekkhā, cittassekaggatā. 
Idaṃ vuccati ‘‘catutthaṃ jhānaṃ’’. 
Avasesā dhammā jhānasampayuttā.
