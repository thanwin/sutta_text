Rūpāvacarakusalaṃ
Catukkanayo
160. Katame dhammā kusalā? 
Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi vivicca akusalehi dhammehi savitakkaṃ savicāraṃ vivekajaṃ pītisukhaṃ paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti …pe… 
ime dhammā kusalā.
