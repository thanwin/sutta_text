166. Katamo tasmiṃ samaye saṅkhārakkhandho hoti? 
Phasso cetanā cittassekaggatā saddhindriyaṃ vīriyindriyaṃ satindriyaṃ samādhindriyaṃ paññindriyaṃ jīvitindriyaṃ sammādiṭṭhi sammāvāyāmo …pe… 
paggāho avikkhepo; 
ye vā pana tasmiṃ samaye aññepi atthi paṭiccasamuppannā arūpino dhammā ṭhapetvā vedanākkhandhaṃ ṭhapetvā saññākkhandhaṃ ṭhapetvā viññāṇakkhandhaṃ – 
ayaṃ tasmiṃ samaye saṅkhārakkhandho hoti …pe… 
ime dhammā kusalā.
Catukkanayo.
