302. Katamo tasmiṃ samaye sammāvāyāmo hoti? 
Yo tasmiṃ samaye cetasiko vīriyārambho nikkamo parakkamo uyyāmo vāyāmo ussāho ussoḷhī thāmo dhiti asithilaparakkamatā anikkhittachandatā anikkhittadhuratā dhurasampaggāho vīriyaṃ vīriyindriyaṃ vīriyabalaṃ sammāvāyāmo vīriyasambojjhaṅgo maggaṅgaṃ maggapariyāpannaṃ – 
ayaṃ tasmiṃ samaye sammāvāyāmo hoti.
