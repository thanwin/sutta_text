Idampi aṭṭhakkhattukaṃ
244. Katame dhammā kusalā? 
Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti ajjhattaṃ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati appamāṇāni suvaṇṇadubbaṇṇāni, tāni abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmīti vivicceva kāmehi …pe… 
paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti …pe… 
avikkhepo hoti …pe… 
ime dhammā kusalā.
