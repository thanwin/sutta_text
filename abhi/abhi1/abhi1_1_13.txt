13. Katamaṃ tasmiṃ samaye vīriyindriyaṃ hoti? 
Yo tasmiṃ samaye cetasiko vīriyārambho nikkamo parakkamo uyyāmo vāyāmo ussāho ussoḷhī thāmo dhiti asithilaparakkamatā anikkhittachandatā anikkhittadhuratā dhurasampaggāho vīriyaṃ vīriyindriyaṃ vīriyabalaṃ sammāvāyāmo – 
idaṃ tasmiṃ samaye vīriyindriyaṃ hoti.
