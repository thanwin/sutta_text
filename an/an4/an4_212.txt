2. Diṭṭhisuttaṃ
212. ‘‘Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye. 
Katamehi catūhi? 
Kāyaduccaritena, vacīduccaritena, manoduccaritena, micchādiṭṭhiyā – 
imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye.
‘‘Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge. 
Katamehi catūhi ? 
Kāyasucaritena, vacīsucaritena, manosucaritena, sammādiṭṭhiyā – 
imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge’’ti. 
Dutiyaṃ.
