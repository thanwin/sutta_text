(12) 2. Āpāyikavaggo
1. Āpāyikasuttaṃ
114. ‘‘Tayome , bhikkhave, āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāya. 
Katame tayo? 
Yo ca abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño, yo ca suddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ carantaṃ amūlakena abrahmacariyena anuddhaṃseti, yo cāyaṃ evaṃvādī evaṃdiṭṭhi – 
‘natthi kāmesu doso’ti, so tāya kāmesu pātabyataṃ āpajjati. 
Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāyā’’ti. 
Paṭhamaṃ.
