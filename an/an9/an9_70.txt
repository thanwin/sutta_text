8. Uddhambhāgiyasuttaṃ
70. ‘‘Pañcimāni , bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni. 
Katamāni pañca? 
Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṃ, avijjā – 
imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni.
‘‘Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddhambhāgiyānaṃ saṃyojanānaṃ pahānāya …pe… 
ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā’’ti. 
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
