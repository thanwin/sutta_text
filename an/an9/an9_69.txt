7. Macchariyasuttaṃ
69. ‘‘Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, macchariyāni. 
Katamāni pañca? 
Āvāsamacchariyaṃ, kulamacchariyaṃ, lābhamacchariyaṃ, vaṇṇamacchariyaṃ, dhammamacchariyaṃ – 
imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca macchariyāni.
‘‘Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ macchariyānaṃ pahānāya …pe… 
ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā’’ti. 
Sattamaṃ.
