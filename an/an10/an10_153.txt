9. Anussaritabbasuttaṃ
153. ‘‘Anussaritabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi na anussaritabbañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo’’ti. 
Navamaṃ.
