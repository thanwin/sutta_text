6. Āsevitabbadhammasuttaṃ
194. ‘‘Āsevitabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi nāsevitabbañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, nāsevitabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, nāsevitabbo dhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, āsevitabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, āsevitabbo dhammo’’ti. 
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
