3. Akusalasuttaṃ
136. ‘‘Akusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi kusalañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṃ? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave , akusalaṃ. 
Katamañca, bhikkhave, kusalaṃ? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusala’’nti. 
Tatiyaṃ.
