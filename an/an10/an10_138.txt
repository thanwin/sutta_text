5. Dhammasuttaṃ
138. ‘‘Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo’’ti. 
Pañcamaṃ.
