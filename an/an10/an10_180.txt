3. Kusalasuttaṃ
180. ‘‘Kusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi akusalañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṃ? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, akusalaṃ.
‘‘Katamañca , bhikkhave, kusalaṃ? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusala’’nti. 
Tatiyaṃ.
