4. Sappurisadhammasuttaṃ
192. ‘‘Sappurisadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asappurisadhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo’’ti. 
Catutthaṃ.
