7. Sāvajjasuttaṃ
140. ‘‘Sāvajjañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi anavajjañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo’’ti. 
Sattamaṃ.
