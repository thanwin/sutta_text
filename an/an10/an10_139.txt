6. Sāsavasuttaṃ
139. ‘‘Sāsavañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi anāsavañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo’’ti. 
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
