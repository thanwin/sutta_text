4. Atthasuttaṃ
137. ‘‘Atthañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anatthañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, anattho? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anattho. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, attho? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, attho’’ti. 
Catutthaṃ.
