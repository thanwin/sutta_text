10. Abhabbasuttaṃ
100. ‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. 
Katame dasa? 
Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ – 
ime kho bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ.
‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātuṃ. 
Katame dasa? 
Rāgaṃ, dosaṃ, mohaṃ, kodhaṃ, upanāhaṃ, makkhaṃ, paḷāsaṃ, issaṃ, macchariyaṃ, mānaṃ – 
ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṃ sacchikātu’’nti. 
Dasamaṃ.
Upālivaggo pañcamo.
Tassuddānaṃ –
Kāmabhogī bhayaṃ diṭṭhi, vajjiyamāhituttiyā;
Kokanudo āhuneyyo, thero upāli abhabboti.
Dutiyapaṇṇāsakaṃ samattaṃ.
