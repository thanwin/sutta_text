9. Anussaritabbasuttaṃ
197. ‘‘Anussaritabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi nānussaritabbañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, nānussaritabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, nānussaritabbo dhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo’’ti. 
Navamaṃ.
