3. Saddhammasuttaṃ
147. ‘‘Saddhammañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi asaddhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaddhammo? 
Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… 
micchāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaddhammo. 
Katamo ca, bhikkhave, saddhammo? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, saddhammo’’ti. 
Tatiyaṃ.
