5. Pañcamasuttaṃ
127. ‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā ekantanibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. 
Katame dasa? 
Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… 
sammāvimutti – 
ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā ekantanibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā’’ti. 
Pañcamaṃ.
