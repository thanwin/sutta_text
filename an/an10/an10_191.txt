3. Saddhammasuttaṃ
191. ‘‘Saddhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asaddhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaddhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaddhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, saddhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, saddhammo’’ti. 
Tatiyaṃ.
