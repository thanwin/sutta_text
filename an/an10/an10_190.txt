2. Kaṇhamaggasuttaṃ
190. ‘‘Kaṇhamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi sukkamaggañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukko maggo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukko maggo’’ti. 
Dutiyaṃ.
