6. Āhuneyyasuttaṃ
16. ‘‘Dasayime , bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa. 
Katame dasa? 
Tathāgato arahaṃ sammāsambuddho, paccekabuddho, ubhatobhāgavimutto, paññāvimutto, kāyasakkhī, diṭṭhippatto, saddhāvimutto, saddhānusārī, dhammānusārī, gotrabhū – 
ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa puggalā āhuneyyā …pe… 
anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassā’’ti. 
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
