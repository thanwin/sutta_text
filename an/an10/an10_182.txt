5. Dhammasuttaṃ
182. ‘‘Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo’’ti. 
Pañcamaṃ.
