2. Ariyadhammasuttaṃ
179. ‘‘Ariyadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anariyadhammañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo’’ti. 
Dutiyaṃ.
