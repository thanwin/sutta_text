10. Sacchikātabbasuttaṃ
198. ‘‘Sacchikātabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi na sacchikātabbañca. 
Taṃ suṇātha …pe… 
katamo ca, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipāto …pe… 
micchādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo.
‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo? 
Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe… 
sammādiṭṭhi – 
ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo’’ti. 
Dasamaṃ.
Ariyamaggavaggo catuttho.
