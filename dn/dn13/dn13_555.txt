Evaṃ vutte, vāseṭṭho māṇavo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca – 
‘‘sutaṃ metaṃ, bho gotama, samaṇo gotamo brahmānaṃ sahabyatāya maggaṃ desetī’’ ti. 
‘‘Sādhu no bhavaṃ gotamo brahmānaṃ sahabyatāya maggaṃ desetu ullumpatu bhavaṃ gotamo brāhmaṇiṃ pajanti. 
‘‘Tena hi, vāseṭṭha, suṇāhi; 
sādhukaṃ manasi karohi; 
bhāsissāmī’’ ti. 
‘‘Evaṃ bho’’ ti kho vāseṭṭho māṇavo bhagavato paccassosi.
