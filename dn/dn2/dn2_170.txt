Ajitakesakambalavādo
170. “ekamidāhaṃ, bhante, samayaṃ yena ajito kesakambalo tenupasaṅkamiṃ; 
upasaṅkamitvā ajitena kesakambalena saddhiṃ sammodiṃ.
sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ sāraṇīyaṃ vītisāretvā ekamantaṃ nisīdiṃ. 
ekamantaṃ nisinno kho ahaṃ, bhante, ajitaṃ kesakambalaṃ etadavocaṃ — 
‘yathā nu kho imāni, bho ajita, puthusippāyatanāni ... pe ... 
sakkā nu kho, bho ajita, evameva diṭṭheva dhamme sandiṭṭhikaṃ sāmaññaphalaṃ paññapetun’ ti?
