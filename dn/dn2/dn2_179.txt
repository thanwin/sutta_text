Sañcayabelaṭṭhaputtavādo
179. “ekamidāhaṃ, bhante, samayaṃ yena sañcayo belaṭṭhaputto tenupasaṅkamiṃ; 
upasaṅkamitvā sañcayena belaṭṭhaputtena saddhiṃ sammodiṃ. 
sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ sāraṇīyaṃ vītisāretvā ekamantaṃ nisīdiṃ. 
ekamantaṃ nisinno kho ahaṃ bhante, sañcayaṃ belaṭṭhaputtaṃ etadavocaṃ — 
‘yathā nu kho imāni, bho sañcaya, puthusippāyatanāni ... pe ... 
sakkā nu kho, bho sañcaya, evameva diṭṭheva dhamme sandiṭṭhikaṃ sāmaññaphalaṃ paññapetun’ ti?
