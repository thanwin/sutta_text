‘‘Cha khome, gahapatiputta, ādīnavā jūtappamādaṭṭhānānuyoge. 
Jayaṃ veraṃ pasavati, jino vittamanusocati, sandiṭṭhikā dhanajāni, sabhāgatassa vacanaṃ na rūhati, mittāmaccānaṃ paribhūto hoti, āvāhavivāhakānaṃ apatthito hoti – 
‘akkhadhutto ayaṃ purisapuggalo nālaṃ dārabharaṇāyā’ ti. 
Ime kho, gahapatiputta, cha ādīnavā jūtappamādaṭṭhānānuyoge.
