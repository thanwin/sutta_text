evaṃ vutte, te māṇavakā unnādino uccāsaddamahāsaddā ahesuṃ — “dujjāto kira, bho, ambaṭṭho māṇavo; akulaputto kira, bho, ambaṭṭho māṇavo; dāsiputto kira, bho, ambaṭṭho māṇavo sakyānaṃ. 
ayyaputtā kira, bho, ambaṭṭhassa māṇavassa sakyā bhavanti. 
dhammavādiṃyeva kira mayaṃ samaṇaṃ gotamaṃ apasādetabbaṃ amaññimhā” ti.
