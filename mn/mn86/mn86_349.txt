349. Atha kho coro aṅgulimālo bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi –
‘‘Gacchaṃ vadesi samaṇa ṭhitomhi, Mamañca brūsi ṭhitamaṭṭhitoti;
Pucchāmi taṃ samaṇa etamatthaṃ, Kathaṃ ṭhito tvaṃ ahamaṭṭhitomhī’’ti.
‘‘Ṭhito ahaṃ aṅgulimāla sabbadā, Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṃ;
Tuvañca pāṇesu asaññatosi, Tasmā ṭhitohaṃ tuvamaṭṭhitosī’’ti.
‘‘Cirassaṃ vata me mahito mahesī, Mahāvanaṃ pāpuṇi saccavādī ;
Sohaṃ carissāmi pahāya pāpaṃ , Sutvāna gāthaṃ tava dhammayuttaṃ’’.
Itveva coro asimāvudhañca, Sobbhe papāte narake akiri;
Avandi coro sugatassa pāde, Tattheva naṃ pabbajjaṃ ayāci.
Buddho ca kho kāruṇiko mahesi, Yo satthā lokassa sadevakassa;
‘Tamehi bhikkhū’ti tadā avoca, Eseva tassa ahu bhikkhubhāvoti.
